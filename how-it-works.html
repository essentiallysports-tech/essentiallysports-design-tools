Design Requests

Build a brief the team can act on.

You do not need design language. Tell the team what the design should communicate, where it will be used, and when it is due.

Start a Design Request
  1. 01

    Give the request a clear, searchable name.

  2. 02
    Entities

    Add the people, teams, leagues, or products featured.

  3. 03
    Copy

    Provide the exact words that should appear in the design.

  4. 04
    Deadline

    Say when it is needed and when it publishes.

  5. 05
    References and notes

    Attach useful links, context, and special instructions.
